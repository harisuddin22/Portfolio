About Filezilla

Filezilla is a free and open source FTP solution that lets you easily transfer files between your computer and your website. Whether you need to upload media files, install plugins and themes, or troubleshoot errors, Filezilla can help you with its fast and secure connection.

Filezilla supports FTP, FTPS, and SFTP protocols, and works on Windows, Mac, and Linux platforms. You can also use Filezilla Pro to access additional cloud storage services, such as Google Drive, Dropbox, Amazon S3, and more.

Filezilla is developed by a community of volunteers who are passionate about creating a reliable and user-friendly FTP client. You can join the Filezilla project and contribute to its development, documentation, translation, or support. You can also donate to the project and help us keep Filezilla free and open source.

To learn more about Filezilla, you can visit the official website¹, read the user guide², or browse the forums³. You can also follow us on social media and stay updated with the latest news and features.

Thank you for choosing Filezilla as your FTP solution. We hope you enjoy using it as much as we enjoy creating it.
