About Shahida

Shahida is an online clothing store that offers high-quality, affordable, and fashionable clothes for women of all sizes and styles.

Our Story

Shahida was founded in 2023 by Shahida Khan, a passionate fashion lover who wanted to create a platform for women to express themselves through their outfits. She started by selling her own designs on social media, and soon gained a loyal customer base. She then partnered with local suppliers and manufacturers to expand her product range and offer more variety and quality. Today, Shahida has over 10,000 customers across Pakistan and beyond, and is one of the leading online clothing stores in the country.

Our Vision

Shahida is constantly evolving to meet the needs and preferences of our customers. We regularly update our collections with the latest trends and styles, and we also offer custom-made orders for special occasions. We are also working on launching a mobile app and a loyalty program to enhance our customer experience and satisfaction.

Our Inspiration

Shahida’s ‘aha!’ moment came when Shahida Khan received a message from a customer who thanked her for making her feel confident and beautiful in her clothes. She realized that her website was not just about selling clothes, but about empowering women and making them happy.

Our Customers

Shahida serves women who love fashion and want to look good without breaking the bank. We offer clothes for all occasions, from casual to formal, from everyday to festive. We also have a wide range of sizes, from XS to XXL, and we provide free shipping and easy returns for our customers.

Our Products

Shahida offers a variety of products, such as dresses, tops, skirts, pants, jackets, accessories, and more. You can browse our catalog and shop online at our website, or you can contact us for any inquiries or requests. We guarantee that you will find something that suits your taste and budget.

We hope you enjoy shopping with us, and we look forward to hearing from you soon. Thank you for choosing Shahida!
